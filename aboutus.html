About Us

Who We Are & What We Do?

Petal Paradise is a globally recognized flower shop based in Sri Lanka, dedicated to bringing the beauty and elegance of our country’s finest flowers to customers around the world. With a deep passion for floriculture, we specialize in exporting a diverse range of flowers, including roses, daisies, tulips, sunflowers, lavender, lilies, snapdragons, daffodils, orchids, anthuriums, carnations, and irises. Whether it’s a joyous celebration, a heartfelt expression, or a simple gesture to brighten someone’s day, our flowers are carefully selected to create unforgettable moments.

At Petal Paradise, quality is our hallmark. Our flowers are nurtured with care, ensuring they are natural, fresh, and of the highest standard. Our extensive network of nurseries, combined with state-of-the-art packing facilities strategically located near major transport hubs, allows us to efficiently export our flowers both locally and globally. We take pride in delivering flowers that are as fresh as the moment they were picked, ensuring that our customers receive only the best.

Our customers span across the globe, with our flowers reaching countries like the Netherlands, Germany, the UK, Japan, and several Middle Eastern nations, including Dubai, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Jordan, and Oman. We are committed to meeting the unique needs of each customer, offering customized floral solutions and ensuring on-time delivery with exceptional service.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide our customers with the finest, freshest flowers and reliable service, while promoting environmental sustainability. We strive to make the world more beautiful and green through our natural products and to enhance the standard of living in Sri Lanka by creating employment opportunities and contributing to the country’s economy through our global exports.

Our vision is to become Sri Lanka’s leading flower shop, renowned for our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and the artistry of floral design. We aspire to be the top choice for customers worldwide, known for delivering exquisite flowers that create lasting memories.
