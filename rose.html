Usage Instructions for Maintaining Freshness

To ensure your roses stay as beautiful as the day they arrive, follow these simple care steps:

  • Unpack Carefully:Gently remove the roses from their packaging upon arrival. Be cautious not to damage the delicate petals.
  • Trim the Stems: Using a sharp, clean pair of scissors or pruning shears, cut the stems at a 45-degree angle. Trimming them under running water can help prevent air from entering the stems, ensuring better water absorption.
  • Prepare the Vase: Fill a clean vase with fresh, cool water and add the flower food provided in your package. Stir until the food is fully dissolved to nourish the roses and extend their life.
  • Arrange Thoughtfully: Place the roses in the vase, ensuring that no leaves are submerged in the water, as this can lead to decay. Arrange them in a way that allows each rose to stand out.
  • Ideal Placement: Keep your vase in a cool, shaded area away from direct sunlight, heating vents, or drafts. This will help preserve the roses’ color and freshness.
